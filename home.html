About D-FOOD

D-FOOD is a professional food donation platform that helps NGOs, donors and receivers collaborate with trust, clarity and accountability.

Mission

Reduce food waste while ensuring nutritious meals reach the people who need them most.

Vision

Create a connected ecosystem where no one goes hungry and every donation is handled responsibly.

Impact

From daily meals to reliable support, D-FOOD helps turn generosity into measurable impact.

How It Works

1. Register

Donors and receivers create their profile and get verified by the admin.

2. Share or Request

Donors list food items and receivers request the food they need.

3. Track Delivery

Admins approve, complete and record delivery progress in a transparent workflow.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does approval work?

Admin review ensures that only verified donor and receiver accounts participate in the platform.

Can I request food instantly?

Yes. Once approved, receivers can submit food requests directly from available donations.

Is the platform secure?

Yes. D-FOOD uses secure session handling and parameterized database operations.
